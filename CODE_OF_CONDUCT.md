# Code of Conduct

## Community Standards
We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all. 

## Expected Behaviors
- Be respectful: Treat all community members with kindness and consideration.
- Be inclusive: Encourage participation from everyone, regardless of backgrounds.
- Be constructive: Provide helpful feedback and support.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment: Any form of harassment or offensive comments.
- Discrimination: Discriminatory jokes or language, or any form of exclusion.
- Intimidation: Threats of violence or disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement Procedures
1. If you see something unacceptable, speak up. You can report to the community moderators.
2. Community moderators will review reports and take action, which may include warnings or temporary bans.
